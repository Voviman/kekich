WE LOVE MAKE TRUE PIZZA

It all started with a love of Neapolitan pizza

The three of us came up with RONI. Three people of different professions and interests who were united by only two things: friendship and love for Neapolitan pizza. We asked ourselves: "Why not create a pizzeria, which we ourselves have always lacked?". And they created it by investing all their free time, many years of business experience, but most importantly - their soul. We lacked a pizzeria where they prepare the right Neapolitan pizza, which is available to everyone. Where the service is lightning fast, but without fuss. Where sincere friendliness reigns and everyone does what they love. Where everyone understands that pizza is much more than food. It wasn't easy, but now such a pizzeria exists.

We are waiting for a visit

Do you still think that only in Naples you can try Neapolitan pizza? Then take a look at one of our pizzerias in Tashkent and see for yourself that any distances are subject to real pizza!
